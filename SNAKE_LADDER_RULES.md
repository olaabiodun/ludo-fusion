# Snake and Ladder - Game Rules

Welcome to the premium Snake and Ladder experience! Below are the rules and mechanics for this version of the game.

## 1. Objective
The goal is to be the first player to reach **Cell 100** (the Champion Cell).

## 2. Gameplay & Movement
- **Starting**: All players begin at position 0 (off the board).
- **Rolling the Dice**: On your turn, tap the 3D dice to roll. Your token will move forward step-by-step according to the number rolled.
- **Turn Order**: The game proceeds in a clockwise sequence:
  - **Yellow** (Top Right)
  - **Red** (Bottom Right)
  - **Blue** (Bottom Left)
  - **Green** (Top Left)
- **Random Starter**: The first player to roll at the beginning of each match is chosen at random.

## 3. Board Features
- **Ladders**: Landing on the bottom of a ladder allows you to climb instantly to the top, skipping several cells.
- **Snakes**: Landing on a snake's head will cause you to slide down its body to the tail, setting you back.

## 4. Winning the Game
- You must land **exactly** on Cell 100 to win. 
- If your dice roll is higher than the number of steps needed to reach 100, your token will stay in its current position and your turn ends.

## 5. Penalties & Timeouts
- **Turn Timer**: Each player has **15 seconds** to roll the dice.
- **Lives System**: Every player starts with **4 lives** (the red pips next to the avatar).
- **Timeout Penalty**: If you fail to roll before your timer runs out, you lose **1 life** and your turn is automatically passed to the next player.

## 6. Controls
- **Rolling**: Tap the floating 3D dice when it glides to your corner.
- **Exit**: Use the back button in the top-left corner to quit, but be aware that leaving a match early results in an automatic forfeit.

Good luck and have fun!
